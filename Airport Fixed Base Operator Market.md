# The Airport Fixed Base Operator (FBO) market plays a pivotal role in the aviation industry by offering a range of essential services to private and commercial aircraft. FBOs serve as the gateway for aircraft operations, providing services such as fueling, hangaring, maintenance, catering, ground handling, and passenger amenities. As of recent analysis up until 2022, the global Airport FBO market had been experiencing steady growth due to increasing air travel demand, expansion of business aviation, and rising investments in airport infrastructure.
